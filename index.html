THE ILLUSION OF ORDER

Function initially led Man to domesticate the environment. Emotion brought Man back to Nature. The illusion of order is reinforces in Cream Collection: a family of three lighting elements with a prismatic, simple and minimalistic design that easily blends into public parks, urban jungles or home gardens. The single identity of the collection - the warm gold glow, the contrasting dark exterior, the embrace of negative space - allows for a harmonious integration into any environment.

S - M - L

Cream S, a cube that reflects light with a different intensity in the front and back, carefully olluminates singular special elements within the space. Cream M casts its golden, beacon-like glow in an ambient hue across its two planes, lighting both sides of the luminaire. Cream L's dramatic size yet humble footprint bathes the open space around it in light, while still retaining the airy feel of the Cream set.

FUNCTION AND EMOTIONS

Cream collection incorporates three lighthing elements with a prismatic, simple and minimalist design that easily blend into parks, open spaces and private gardens. Cream L is a column measuring 3.20 metres in height and is supplemented by beacon Cream M measuring 74 cm; both with identical geometric features and lighting conditions. Cream S is a beacon in the shape of a 25 cm cube. Cream shows us the link between two apparently opposite genres: prose and poetry, function and emotion.
